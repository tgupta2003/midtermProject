About | LeadHer

We aim to create a safe, inspiring, and effective space for girls in CS to reach out for guidance on their career development and personal growth.

Roughly only 20% of computer science undergraduate degrees in the United States are recieved by women annually.

In an isolating field, networking, getting through class, and facing the industry can be challenging and intimidating. The journey towards where we want to be is a long and often hard one -- lets start by getting connected and learning, sharing, and growing from other girls' experiences!
