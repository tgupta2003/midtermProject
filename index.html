A women-in-computer science mentroship crafted for you.

Hey there! Do you identify as a girl, are interested in or are currently pursing a CS major/minor, and go to NYU? LeadHer is the a mentorship platform where girls in CS @NYU partner up with a girl mentor (a junior, senior, or graduate under 25) from NYU for guidance on their career development. In a typically intimidating environment lacking a strong female presence, your journey towards independence and growth in this field just got bit smoother...

Advice seeker, eager to learn, be prepared, work on personal growth, take risks, explore their passions, is curious about tech industry, and is patient.

Provides direction, shares personal experiences, their growth as a student, valuable life lessons, detailed look into professional opportunities, and insight into industry experiences.
